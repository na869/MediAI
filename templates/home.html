Core Abstract

Healthcare systems generate vast clinical datasets. Our system implements a predictive analytics framework using supervised machine learning to assist in medication selection. By analyzing biometric vectors (Age, BP, Cholesterol, Na/K Ratio), the CDSS identifies probable pathological conditions and suggests validated therapeutic regimens, functioning as a vital support tool for practitioners in limited-resource environments.

Algorithmic Blueprints

Weighted Ensemble Core

The system utilizes a majority-voting consensus between Random Forest, Decision Tree, and KNN. Each prediction is cross-validated across these three distinct architectures to ensure institutional-grade precision.

Biometric Vectors

Processing Age, Gender, BP class, and the critical Na-to-K ratio in real-time.

XAI Attribution

Neural decision factors mapped via Explainable AI to justify every suggestion.

Clinical Reliability

Automated disease inference from symptomatic data minimizes human bias, providing a secondary clinical opinion based on historical patient outcomes and statistical consensus.
